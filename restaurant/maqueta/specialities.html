にぎり

Sake

  • Junmai

    This pure sake is made with only rice, water, yeast and koji.

  • Ginjo

    Ginjo sake is characterized by its fermentation process at lower temperatures and a fruity and floral flavor.

  • Daiginjo

    A premium sake that is made with highly polished rice, resulting in a smooth and elegant flavor with fruity and floral notes.

  • Nigori

    This sake is not fully filtered, giving it a cloudy appearance and a sweet, creamy flavor with a hint of rice.

  • Taruzake

    This sake is aged in cedar barrels, giving it a distinctive flavor and earthy notes.

  • Honjozo

    A traditional-style sake with a longer fermentation process, giving it a bolder, more umami flavor.

  • Tokubetsu

    Aromatic fresh herbs such as peppermint, mixed with citrus nuances

  • Koshu

    This sake is produced with special care and is often blended with unique ingredients to obtain distinctive flavors, such as hints of fruit or spice.

  • Namazake

    An aged sake, which acquires deeper and more complex nuances over time.
