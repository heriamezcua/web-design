金田財団

Kaneda foundation

A century ago, in the heart of Tokyo, Kaneda was born, a modest Japanese restaurant that, over time, has become a culinary icon of the city and an international benchmark. Founded in 1923 by chef Takeshi Kaneda, this establishment has maintained an unbreakable tradition of excellence in preparing authentic Japanese dishes. From humble beginnings, Kaneda has cultivated a welcoming atmosphere that seamlessly fuses Japanese elegance with the hospitality of its staff. Over the years, her reputation has been built on her commitment to quality, using fresh ingredients and traditional culinary techniques.

長い歴史を持つ料理の伝統

A long culinary heritage

Since its founding, the restaurant has maintained a close relationship with its suppliers to guarantee the quality of the ingredients. Kaneda chefs have dedicated themselves to perfecting their culinary skills through constant training and mastery of traditional Japanese cooking techniques.

Adversities have only strengthened their commitment to culinary excellence. In each dish, each flavor, and each presentation, there is testimony to a story of determination, passion and the search for perfection. Kaneda persists as a beacon of Japanese haute cuisine.

美食の目的地

A gastronomic destination

Today, Kaneda Restaurant continues to thrive and maintain its status as one of the most respected dining destinations in Japan and around the world. Its focus on quality, authenticity and innovation has allowed it to maintain a loyal clientele and attract visitors from all over seeking an unparalleled culinary experience.

Additionally, Kaneda has embraced sustainable trends, incorporating environmentally friendly practices into its operation, which has generated greater appreciation from a clientele increasingly aware of the importance of sustainability.
