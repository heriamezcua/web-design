Journey

We have come a long way

2025

MAKING HISTORY

Procyon has gained worldwide attention for a series of historic milestones. It is the only private company capable of returning a spacecraft from moon-to-earth, and in 2025 our Scorpio spacecraft became the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to and from the Moon.

2027

FIRST COMMERCIAL TRIP

This year we became the first private space company to carry out a commercial orbital trip. The mission was a success making Procyon and its Light Eagle rocket model known worldwide.

2029

THE HELIUM ROAD

In 2029 we established a new commercial route, the helium route, where a constant connection is maintained with the space stations located at the north pole of the moon. Making normal life on the moon possible.

2032

NEXT STOP : MARS

Currently, we are working on the most important mission not only of Procyon but of humanity. Establish a colony on Mars and achieve one of humanity's greatest milestones: becoming an interplanetary species.
