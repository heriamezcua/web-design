01  /  HISTORY

A incredible cultural heritage

333 BD

The conquest of Alexander

Alexander the Great's impact on what is now Türkiye was significant. During his conquests in the 4th century BC, he incorporated the region of Anatolia into his empire, leaving a cultural and architectural legacy. He founded cities, promoted the fusion of cultures, and his influence endured in the region even after his death. This period marked the beginning of the Hellenization of Anatolia and its lasting connection with Greek culture.

63 BD

Roman domination

The Roman Empire had a significant impact. For centuries, Turkey was a crucial part of the Roman Empire, with cities like Ephesus and Antioch becoming important centers. The region experienced Roman cultural influence, including architecture, government, and the spread of Christianity. The Christianization of Turkey was especially relevant, as it became one of the centers of early Christianity. The Roman heritage in Turkey is evident in numerous archaeological sites.

1453 AD

The Ottoman Empire

The Ottoman Empire had a significant impact on Turkey, shaping its history, culture and society for several centuries. The empire, which was established in the 13th century, played a crucial role in the region and beyond. His legacy includes the introduction of the Ottoman political, legal and administrative system, as well as the influence on architecture, art and language.
